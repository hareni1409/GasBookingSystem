MUST DO'S

  • The cylinder cap and key must be in place when handling the cylinders.
  • Keep in secure and upright position.
  • Turn off the knob on the gas cylinder after use.

MUST DONT'S

  • ☓ Don't roll cylinders horizontally on the ground.
  • ☓ Don't use grease or oil on valves.
  • ☓ Don’t transport or shift or move cylinder without putting valve safety cap.
